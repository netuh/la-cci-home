The IEEE Latin American Conference on Computational Intelligence (LA-CCI) is a conference sponsored by the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). IEEE is a global professional association that brings together students, professors, professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts in the field of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from around the world, playing a significant role in advancing technological innovation. With a rich history spanning several decades, IEEE promotes the scientific and technological development of various disciplines, including Computer Science, by fostering collaboration, disseminating knowledge, and organizing conferences and events of international significance. By emphasizing the importance of ethics and social responsibility, IEEE contributes to the education and professional growth of individuals in the field, while also making valuable contributions to the society at large.

Registration will be handled by the EasyChair system. However, it is recommended to carefully read the information below to know the conditions and deadlines related to the registration. All registration fees are in American Dollar (US$/USD).

A full registration allows the presentation of up to 2 papers.

To be included in the conference, a paper requires at least a full-registration and to be presented in the conference (oral or poster). The paper will be included in the proceedings only if at least one of the authors is fully registered in the conference.

The benefits of the registration fee include:

  • Access to all technical sessions, including IEEE LA-CCI and IEEE CIS SSoCIA
  • Access to the exhibition area
  • Conference proceedings
  • Participant materials (bag, booklet, pen, USB drive)
  • Coffee breaks and banquet
  • Social events

Additional Information

There will be a registration fee for students. It is available only available to those who are currently enrolled in undergraduate or graduate courses at Higher Education Institutions and do not apply to post-doc researchers. An electronic proof of the student status with affiliation, participant's name, course which he/she is enrolled at, and current date will be required. The proof must be sent to the registration system. Authors of papers accepted for presentation at the conference must register as full participants.
