Prof. Patricia Takako Endo

University of Pernambuco, Pernambuco, Brazil

Patricia Takako Endo is currently an Associate Professor at the University of Pernambuco. She obtained her PhD in Computer Science from the Federal University of Pernambuco (UFPE) and did her postdoctoral work at Dublin City University (DCU), Ireland. She is a lead researcher at the dotLAB Brazil research group and has been developing projects in Artificial Intelligence & Health.
