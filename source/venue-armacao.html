IEEE LA-CCI 2023 will take place at Armação Hotel Porto de Galinhas, located 50km from the Guararapes International Airport, Recife, and 2km from the village of Porto de Galinhas.

  1. Was the venue for the 2013 BRICS Congress on Computational Intelligence & 11th Brazilian Congress on Computational Intelligence (BRICS-CCI & CBIC) with roughly 600 attendees
  2. Has the necessary structure for conferences which includes 2 (two) convention centres and 18 (eighteen) rooms with 2,500 people;
  3. Is located in the center of Porto de Galinhas with easy access when compared to others Venues such as Nannai Hotel;
  4. Was elected among the 25th best hostels in Brazil according to the Travelers Choice 2022 and 3rd best in Porto de Galinhas

Accommodation

The available infrastructure allows you to schedule sessions in parallel. Wi-Fi access is open.

At Armação Resort Porto de Galinhas there are 214 apartments. It was rated by Tripadvisor as the number one hotel for business in Porto de Galinhas and four stars. We propose to work with an event organizing agency that will provide a list of hotels with especially reduced rates. There are hotels, hostels and inns in the same area of the city of the venue, and several of them within a walking distance of the Armação Resort Porto de Galinhas (the hotels close to the venue are shown in the next map). Additionally, it is possible to get around on foot, taxis and as well as transport apps are available at reasonable prices.
